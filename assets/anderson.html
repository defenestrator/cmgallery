Like her work Sheila Anderson was one of a kind. She has left behind a bounty of beautiful
jewelry.
Sheila Anderson Studios created one of a kind, jewelry designs inspired by nature. The artful use
of natural gems from the earth, and crystals in her designs result in unique statement pieces.
These pieces are designed for special occasions and any time as a personal expression. In
addition, Sheila had studied the healing properties of natural stones, and created pieces that
possess healing properties for the benefit of overall wellness. She will truly be missed.
